Our Story

There is only one happiness in this life, to love and be loved -- George Sand

  • Started From Way Back

    Biodun and I have been best of friends for over 5 years. Biodun has always been my personal wing man, the first person I tell when I meet someone new, when I have a bad date or when I break up with a boyfriend. I was also his wing girl. Always matchmaking him with hot girls and looking out for him in his relationships. Everyone around found it funny that we never thought to date ourselves as we were so comfortable being just friends and looking out for each other for years.

    When I eventually moved to the UK to further my studies, He never stopped communicating with me. OMG! He would leave messages, voicemails, voice notes till I ring him back. Most of my friends thought I wasn’t being honest about my relationship with him but I was just so comfortable being his bestie. He was the one good guy I could trust and I didn’t want to ruin that except I was sure he was truly “The One”.

    I knew I liked him but I was in denial for a very long time until one very day, he sent me a message saying “we need to talk about something serious”. He called me later and said

    “Efe how would you like to be my girlfriend?”

    I was lost for words after he told me how he really felt about me and the rest they say is history (lol). He eventually won me over with not only his charm and caring nature but also his love for God and his passion to see me fulfill my dreams. I am so excited about spending the rest of my life with my vey own Mr Perfect, my gisting partner, the love of my life and my best friend.


    -- Efe

  • I Found My Soul ...

    I met Efe at my work place where she came for a short service and NYSC. We were in different departments and usually see from time to time. On a Friday she came into my department and she got talking with me and some of my colleges. We clicked almost immediately. She later returned to do her National Service in my office and this time she was in the same department.

    We grew closer by the day and eventually became best of friends. She eventually travelled out to further her studies abroad. I thought that will be the end of our friendship but that wasn’t the case. The friendship grew and communication increased significantly that I started falling in love with her gradually to the extent that talking to her became a part of my routine.

    Early this year, I made my intentions known to her. I told her I had fallen in love with her and I would love to spend the rest of my life with her. She was shocked and dumbfounded. Fast forward, in April when she visited Nigeria we got to see each other and that same day I proposed marriage to her.


    -- Biodun
